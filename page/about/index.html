About me

Who is this masked man?

Preet Singh is rumoured to know fourteen languages, eleven of them of his own invention. We can’t find any mention of him dating back more than five years, though to be fair, our pizza arrived so we stopped looking.

What we do know is that he enjoys algorithmic strategy, writes code and occasionally calls himself a scientist because that impresses his niece. He received his undergraduate degree in Computer Science & Economics, and dropped out of a PhD in Algorithmic Economics. He has research publications in algorithm design and multiagent systems AI, and hopes to one day have a research paper here.
